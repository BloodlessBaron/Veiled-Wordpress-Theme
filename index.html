Veiled Beyond Banality

Welcome! You've reached the landing page for my personal site, wayward traveller. Choose now the path you will take in this, your journey beyond the veil of the banal.

You might take the path that leads to my journals, my personal accounting of my own travels in this strange and sometimes perilous web of information they call the Internet.

Or you might decide to peruse my tomes of knowledge, the arcane texts I have developed over the years. With the correct instruments, these modest tools of thaumaturgy might allow you to perform works that will astound the ignorant minds of the masses.

Another path you could take is to view the myriad pieces of artwork I have created. While I have no great skill, I am proud of them, and hope you will find them as pleasing to the eyes as I do.

Finally, if you must contact me, and have access to enchanted mail, you might send me a scroll with your query, and I will respond to you as soon as I am able to.
